About EHR Logic

EHR Logic is a HealthTech Software Solution company that provides interoperability solutions to the Indian healthcare industry and is working on the compliance process with the ABDM ecosystem.

At EHR Logic, our mission is to revolutionize the healthcare industry by providing a secure and efficient healthcare information exchange platform that enables seamless communication and collaboration among healthcare providers, patients, and payers, with the ultimate goal of improving public health.

As a socially responsible company, we believe that access to quality healthcare is a fundamental human right, and we are dedicated to making a positive impact on public health. We strive to build strong partnerships with healthcare providers, government agencies, and other stakeholders to create a more efficient and effective healthcare system that benefits everyone.
