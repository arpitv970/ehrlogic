About

e-BloodConnect is a fully compliant Blood Bank solution to digitalise and streamline the management of blood banks with Donor Registration, medical Screening, automated inventory control to provide greater efficiency and improving patient safety. Our blood bank solution can automate the process of labelling and tracking blood products as they are processed and distributed. This ensures that blood products are properly labelled and tracked throughout the supply chain, from donation to transfusion. Another important feature of a blood bank solution is the ability to manage the testing and screening of blood donations. The software can track the results of donor screening tests, including infectious disease testing, and can flag any potential issues or concerns.

Vision

eBloodConnect solutions are software applications that enable blood banks and transfusion services to manage their operations effectively

“To ensure Availability and Accessibility of safe and secure blood on time”
