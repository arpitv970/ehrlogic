Colonel B.S. Rathore

Dr. Himmat Singh

Designation : Senior Advisor, Payas Dairy Qualification & Experience : Masters in Veterinary (1973), RCDF / Mother Dairy / Reliance Expertise : Master the art of Deploying large scale Public Welfare Programs.

Dr. Himmat Singh is Bachelor of Veterinary Science from Bikaner. He did his Masters in Veterinary Science from Udaipur in 1973 and joined Rajasthan Cooperative Dairy Federation (RCDF) in 1975. During his more than 25 years of service in RCDF , he held various key positions in various milk unions and elevated to position of Managing Director, Jaipur Dairy, the largest milk union of RCDF. He has also worked as Additional Managing Director of Mother Dairy Delhi.

He has served as state head in Reliance Dairy Food Ltd., Jaipur. In addition he was associated with Payas Milk Producer Company Ltd., which is the first Farmer Milk Producer Company incorporated under guidance of NDDB and NDDB Dairy Services.

Dr. Singh has also made contribution in growth, research and education of Dairy industry. He pioneered milk procurement system on Anand Pattern model in Jaipur District under Rajasthan State Dairy Development Corporation in 1975 and introduced Artificial Insemination program under operation flood in 1977. He has also set up New Generation Cooperative system of milk procurement in Mother Dairy, Delhi. He played a major role in conceptualising and implementing the Producer Company concept of NDDB and trained manpower in various Producer organisations for establishment operations in India.
